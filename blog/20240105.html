「正義」は生き続ける。

5 Jan 2024


This is Emmannuel Ortega, Social & Web Manager for #MamaNyoSquad.

We are almost at the end of an era as SINoALICE bids farewell for the final moment for everyone playing on the Japanese Edition. We at the Squad had been tuning in as the news unfolds to the rest of the nation.

As a fellow player, I understood what the others feel as they mourn the loss of another great game that has touched the hearts of the many; however, may we utilize this moment to cherish our past experiences as we usher forward to the brighter future.

I would like to extend my thanks to SQUARE ENIX and Pokelabo for keeping the spirit alive to its last breath. Without your support, there will be no SINoALICE as we know it today. The accolades both of you had shared to the players will always linger on forever.

To Yoko Taro, who have been the brains behind this project: As Nana Bajo, you have my sincerest thanks in everything that you do for SINoALICE. I hope that you will be able to usher forward with your new projects as well as revolutionizing things that captured everyone's hearts to the fullest.

For the final moment, before the Farewell Special: Godspeed, SINoALICE!
