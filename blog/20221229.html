End of Support for Windows 8.1 -- what does it mean for DOAXVV?

29 Dec 2022


General Information about the End of Support

Microsoft published this article indicating the EoS Notice for Windows 8.1 Operating System (all editions).

This won't mean that the OS will not function on your PC anymore, but will no longer receive future updates. Some apps and games will no longer work beyond the support period as well.

And unlike Windows 7, Microsoft will NOT provide the Extended Security Update (ESU) program for this operating system.

KOEI TECMO's Decision

KOEI TECMO GAMES Co., Ltd. and Team NINJA have decided that starting from 10 January 2023 JST (and not PST due to respecting timezones), DEAD OR ALIVE Xtreme Venus Vacation will no longer be supported on Windows 8.1. In addition, future updates to the client will also be ceased.

KT urges the customers to upgrade their PC to a newer operating system, beginning with Windows 10.

What does it mean for everyone playing DOAXVV?

This will mean that players who are playing the game will have only little time to upgrade their PC to Windows 10 or above, as most features will be disabled.

For those who are playing on Windows 10 or above, this will not be an issue; but it's recommended to update the system quite often to stay updated on the fixes client-wide and system-wide.

System Requirements Post-EoS

Here are the following (recommended) Updated System Requirements if you want to play DOAXVV:

  • OS: Windows 10 or higher
  • Frame Rate: 30/60 FPS
  • RAM: 2GB or higher
  • CPU: Intel Core i5-3470 and/or equivalents (30 FPS), Intel Core i3-3225 and/or equivalents (60 FPS), or higher
  • GPU: AMD RADEON R7 260X GB (30 FPS), NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 2GB and/or equivalents (60FPS), or higher
  • Resolution: 1080p Widescreen (1920x1080)
  • Storage: 20GB minimum
  • Sound: Anything that's compatible with DirectX 9.0c or higher
  • Network: Broadband internet, and a platform account (DMM, Steam, Johren)
  • Peripherals: Mouse or similar pointing device
