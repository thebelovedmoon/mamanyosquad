「Justice」continues to live on.

2 Sep 2023


This is Emmannuel Ortega, Social & Web Manager for #MamaNyoSquad.

I have been a part of the SINoALICE universe since its inception on July 2020, and since then I have been committed to enriching its experience through my expertise. This game have been a major factor in bringing the universes together for a never-ending desire, something that I have been looking forward to since its inception.

It began when one of the closest people I associate myself with has introduced me to it, and she has ushered her own efforts along with it. That gave me an interest in trying that out, and soon I became hooked on to it. Although we could not talk about it anymore since she has since been disappeared without a trace. However, her spirit will always linger with me and I will continue her journey through countless efforts and determination through this game.

And in addition, she became one of what I call the Circles, something that I will be cherishing for generations to come. That is why I founded the 「Seal of the Circle (SotC)」 to repay my gratitude towards two of the people whom I associate with, that I loved deeply in my heart.

My story had become livelier when I realized one of the characters in the game—Snow White—perfectly fits my vision of taking matters with prejudice and consideration, where impartiality becomes the basis for a rational approach to every situation.

And I vowed, moving forward, that I will represent as the endeared Symbol of Justice.

During my experience with SINoALICE, I have a Guild named 「TheLonelyBride」, which initially was attributed to one of the Classes associated with Snow White; specifically the Cleric class, where she endowed herself in an alternative revision of a wedding dress. However, that has changed when she debuted later in her Mage class—something that shocked the community as she endowed in an elegant literal wedding dress itself. This, complete with a stamp titled 「Snow White/Bride」, has made me stuck upon for the remainder of my experiences as a whole, while becoming as one of the Players who represent Snow White.

Those experiences, among other instances that I did not mention in this Message, are what I will be taking with when the Global Edition finally seals the Library into a halt. The memories and experiences I had with it became a foundation that will be inherited by the ideas that I will populate in its honor.

To every Guild that I have fought a battle on in the Colosseum, to every Player whom I had a co-op battle with, to everyone who have stuck with the community to its final breath, I thank you for your never-ending commitment in harnessing the stories from within.

To SQUARE ENIX and Pokelabo: Without both of your efforts, SINoALICE would not have been possible. Your commitment to ensuring quality on your game has been one of the biggest factors in which I loved, and I hope that will carry on with your future endeavors. ありがとうございます。

To those who have read to the very end of this Message, I hope you can support SINoALICE Global to the very end. You can find them on Twitter / X at @sinoaliceglobal, or play the game along with me to the end. Let us wander around the Library for the final time.

And, before I can conclude this Message: I will not be going anywhere. As soon as Pokelabo pulls the plug on Global Edition, I will be around on the Japanese Edition. So, you can expect the same experiences as it would during my first experiences on the former.



「正義」は生き続ける。

SINoALICE Global Edition
