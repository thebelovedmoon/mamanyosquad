PRESS RELEASE

Celebrating 5 Years of DOAXVV Global

19 Apr 2024


This is thebelovedmoon; General Manager of #MamaNyoSquad, and long-time player of DOAXVV Global since .

I first started this game out of curiosity and since I'm bored and have nothing to do, but then it quickly spiraled into one of the games that I play on a daily basis. And I have to say; the more I play the game, the more I appreciate the efforts the Staff put out on this venture -- big or small. It introduced me to a world unlike any other, where different circumstances and experiences collide to form an uncertainty that only we can imagine.

And during those times, in my perspective, I've seen a lot happening with my own eyes; from PC switching to retiring of some in-game features that mattered to the game, I've seen many evolutions that redefine what is DOAXVV at its core without straying away from its intended purpose.

And along the way, it gave me a mission to get the word out about further activities of the game. I mean, we already have (Japanese Edition) to make up for that, but what about its other counterpart? With that in mind, I decided to achieve the impossible -- to give the Global Edition its own platform in places where it hadn't shown its full potential, specifically Twitter (currently X).

With it, I introduced the world to DOAXVV Global through #MamaNyoSquad -- a platform dedicated to DOAXVV, the DOA Series, and beyond.

I have never been more grateful than having to introduce a platform in which both Editions can showcase in harmony, and all of it was thanks to all the hard work of what the DOAXVV Development Staff has produced over the years. It has already became my sanctuary for all my upcoming projects to let the world know more about the quirks of the Series, and how we as fans appreciate more about it.

To it, I send my thanks -- on behalf of the SquadMates -- to the DOAXVV Global Development Staff for providing more than just what was required to appreciate the game at its core. Despite the hurdles, you all go beyond the expectations -- providing us players with more than just swimsuits and event stories, but the connections that matter to the Venuses and everyone in between.

In my perspective, I'm very excited on what you all can build next. I'm looking forward to more exciting days together with the game (even though I suffered many times in the Gacha alone), and wishing everyone all the best!!
